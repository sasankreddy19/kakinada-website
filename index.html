Explore Kakinada

Kakinada, situated on the eastern coast of Andhra Pradesh, India, is a vibrant city known for its pristine beaches, bustling harbor, and rich culture. Home to the picturesque Uppada Beach, Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary, and the scenic Hope Island, Kakinada offers something for every traveler.

The city is also famous for its delectable Andhra cuisine, including the iconic Kakinada Kaaja, and its harmonious blend of natural beauty and urban charm.
